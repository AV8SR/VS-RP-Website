Welcome to Victoria State Roleplay

Realistic Australian ER:LC Roleplay – Friendly. Fair. Fun. Voice Chat Optional.

About Us

Victoria State Roleplay is an immersive Australian ER:LC roleplay community. Our goal is to provide realistic emergency services roleplay in a respectful and inclusive environment. With optional voice chat, you're free to enjoy the game your way.

Server Rules

  • Respect all players and staff.
  • No FailRP, RDM, or VDM.
  • No exploiting, glitching, or cheating.
  • Follow all Roblox ToS and ER:LC rules.
  • Voice Chat is optional but must be used appropriately.
  • Use common sense at all times.

How to Join

  1. Join our Discord Server.
  2. Verify and read the rules.
  3. Request access or apply for departments.
  4. Join our private server in ER:LC and enjoy!
